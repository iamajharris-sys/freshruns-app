Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Fresh Runs ("we," "us," or "our") operates the grocery delivery service available at freshruns.netlify.app. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices. By using Fresh Runs, you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

When you place an order, we collect only what we need to shop for and deliver it:

  • Your name
  • Your delivery address and any gate or access notes
  • Your mobile phone number
  • The items in your order and your payment (processed securely by Stripe — we never see or store your full card number)

How we use your information

  • To shop for, prepare, and deliver your grocery order
  • To send you order and delivery updates by text message (see below)
  • To provide customer support and resolve issues with your order
SMS / text messaging. By providing your mobile number at checkout, you consent to receive transactional text messages from Fresh Runs about your order — including order confirmation, a notification when your order is ready to approve and pay, and delivery updates. Message frequency varies based on your order activity (typically a few messages per order). Message and data rates may apply. You can reply STOP at any time to opt out of text messages, or reply HELP for assistance.
We do not sell or share your mobile number. Your mobile phone number and SMS consent are never sold, rented, or shared with third parties or affiliates for their own marketing or promotional purposes. Mobile information is used solely to operate the Fresh Runs service and deliver your order. No mobile information will be shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

Who we share information with

We share the minimum necessary information only with service providers who help us operate:

  • Stripe — to securely process your payment
  • Twilio — to deliver order and delivery text messages to you
  • Your Fresh Runs shopper and driver — your name, address, and order, so they can fulfill and deliver it

We do not sell your personal information.

Data retention

We keep order information for as long as needed to operate the service, resolve disputes, and meet legal or tax obligations. You may request deletion of your information by contacting us.

Your choices

  • Reply STOP to any text to stop receiving messages.
  • Contact us to access, correct, or delete your information.

Contact us

Questions about this policy or your information? Email hello@freshruns.app.
